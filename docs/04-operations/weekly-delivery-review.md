# Weekly Delivery Review Format

Cadence: Weekly
Audience: Product, Design, Engineering, QA, Platform

## Agenda (45 minutes)

1. Progress vs Tasks.md
2. Completed items and demo highlights
3. Blockers and risk register updates
4. Quality and incident summary
5. Next-week commitment and scope lock

## Required Inputs

- Updated `Tasks.md` status
- Updated risk register
- Test pass/fail summary
- Deployment and incident notes

## Output Artifacts

- Confirmed completed tasks IDs
- Newly accepted scope changes
- Owners and due dates for blockers
- Next-week top 10 tasks
