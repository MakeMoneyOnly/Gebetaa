# Brand Identity Document

## Overview

This document defines the brand identity for Gebeta, a restaurant infrastructure platform designed for emerging markets. The brand identity encompasses visual elements, voice, messaging, and positioning that will be used across all touchpoints.

---

## Brand Foundation

### Brand Name

**Primary Name:** Gebeta

**Pronunciation:** GEH-beh-tah

**Meaning:** "Food" in Amharic (Amharic: ·åå·â§·â≥)

**Name Justification:**
- **Cultural Relevance:** Uses a word from the primary target market language (Amharic)
- **Memorable:** Short, easy to pronounce, unique
- **Meaningful:** Directly relates to the product domain (food/restaurants)
- **Available:** .com domain available (gebeta.com)
- **Scalable:** Works across languages and cultures

### Tagline

**Primary Tagline:** "Smart Menu, Smarter Business"

**Alternative Taglines:**
- "Restaurant Intelligence for Everyone"
- "Your Menu, Reimagined"
- "Data-Driven Dining"

**Tagline Explanation:**
- "Smart Menu" refers to the digital menu with intelligent upselling
- "Smarter Business" emphasizes the business intelligence aspect
- Short, memorable, and differentiates from basic digital menu tools

### Brand Personality

**Primary Personality Traits:**

1. **Approachable** - Easy to use, friendly, welcoming
2. **Intelligent** - Smart, insightful, data-driven
3. **Trustworthy** - Reliable, secure, honest
4. **Innovative** - Forward-thinking, modern, progressive
5. **Local** - Understanding of emerging markets, culturally aware

**Personality Don'ts:**
- Not arrogant or condescending
- Not overly technical or complex
- Not expensive or exclusive
- Not outdated or old-fashioned

**Voice Characteristics:**

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Friendly** | Warm, welcoming, conversational | "Hey there! Let's get your menu online in minutes." |
| **Helpful** | Supportive, guiding, solution-oriented | "Here's how to boost your average order value by 20%." |
| **Confident** | Assured, expert, authoritative | "We've helped 500+ restaurants increase revenue." |

- No corporate jargon or buzzwords

### Imagery

#### Photography Style

**Characteristics:**
- Authentic, real moments (not staged)
- Warm, inviting lighting
- Focus on people and food
- Emerging market contexts
- Diverse representation

**Do's:**
- Use photos from actual restaurant settings
- Show diverse people (age, gender, ethnicity)
- Capture genuine interactions
- Include local context and culture
- Show modern, clean environments

**Don'ts:**
- Don't use stock photography
- Don't show unrealistic or idealized scenes
- Don't use overly professional/staged images
- Don't exclude any demographic groups
- Don't use outdated or culturally insensitive imagery

#### Illustration Style

**Characteristics:**
- Modern, flat vector illustrations
- Friendly, approachable character
- Minimal detail, focus on concept
- Brand color palette
- Custom illustrations

**Usage:**
- Onboarding flows
- Empty states
- Success/completion states
- Feature highlights
- Marketing materials

| **Background** | White (#FFFFFF) | White (#FFFFFF) |

|-----------|-----------|-----------------|

| **Background** | White (#FFFFFF) | White (#FFFFFF) |
| **Ratio Required** | 4.5:1 minimum | 3:1 minimum |

### WCAG Compliance

**Level AA Compliance:**
- Color contrast meets minimum ratios
- Focus states visible on all interactive elements
- Keyboard navigation supported
- Screen reader compatible
- No loss of content when zoomed to 200%
- Error messages clear and identifiable

### Accessibility Features

1. **Keyboard Navigation:** All interactive elements accessible via keyboard
2. **Focus Indicators:** Visible focus rings (2px solid Gebeta Green)
3. **Screen Readers:** ARIA labels on all interactive elements
4. **Color Independence:** Information not conveyed by color alone
5. **Text Scaling:** Supports up to 200% zoom without loss of functionality
6. **Motion Reduction:** Respects reduced motion preferences

## Ethiopia Context Considerations

### Cultural Sensitivity

**Respect Local Culture:**
- Use local examples and case studies
- Show diverse representation in imagery
- Acknowledge local payment methods (Telebirr)
- Respect local business practices
- Use appropriate date/time formats (Ethiopian calendar available)

**Language Considerations:**
- English as primary language
- Amharic translations for UI elements
- Support for right-to-left (RTL) for Arabic
- Clear, simple language (not idiomatic)

**Visual Considerations:**
- Avoid culturally inappropriate imagery
- Use local restaurant contexts in photos
- Show modern Ethiopian settings
- Inclusive representation

---

### Technical Considerations

**Low-End Device Optimization:**
- Lightweight visual assets
- Efficient color usage (avoid gradients, shadows)
- Minimal animations (performance-focused)
- Compressed images (WebP format)

**Connectivity Considerations:**
- Clear offline indicators
- Cached content for slow connections
- Minimal JavaScript payload
- Fast loading on 3G networks
