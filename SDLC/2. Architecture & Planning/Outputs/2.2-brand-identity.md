## 1. Brand Strategy Foundation

### Purpose

To modernize the African dining experience by bridging the gap between traditional hospitality and digital efficiency.

### Value Proposition

**For** Restaurant Owners in emerging markets
**Who** struggle with inflation and operational chaos
**Gebeta is a** Restaurant Operating System
**That provides** real-time control and revenue intelligence
**Unlike** static paper menus or expensive western POS systems.

### Values

1.  **Empowerment:** We give control back to the owners.
2.  **Simplicity:** Technology should be invisible.
3.  **Reliability:** Works when the internet doesn't.
4.  **Craft:** Every pixel and interaction is premium.

## 3. Visual Identity System

### Design Language: "Wild & Vibrant"

- **Dynamic Typography:** Intersection of modern Sans (`Inter`) and classic Serif (`Playfair Display`).
- **Chromatic Energy:** High-contrast palette featuring Brand Crimson and Sunny Yellow against clean white surfaces.
- **Fluid Motion:** Lightweight interactions with snappy transitions (`scale-95`, `duration-300`).
- **Organic Shapes:** Use of scalloped badges and the custom "Filters" burst shape for visual interest.

### Color System

**Primary & Accents**

- `Brand-Crimson`: `#A81818` (Header, Primary Buttons)
- `Brand-Yellow`: `#F2C94C` (Highlight, "Wild" accents)
- `Brand-Blue`: `#1848A8` (Secondary Action)
- `Brand-Mint`: `#E0F2E9` (Promotional Banners)

**Surface & Neutral**

- `Surface-0`: `#FFFFFF` (Main Background)
- `Surface-1`: `#F8F8F8` (Secondary card surfaces)
- `Text-Main`: `#000000`
- `Text-Muted`: `#6B7280`

### Typography

**Font Family:** `Inter` (Sans) & `Playfair Display` (Serif)

- **Inter:** Primary UI font. High weights (900) for headers.
- **Playfair Display:** Emotional accent font. Used in Italic for "wild" styling.

**Weights:**

- **Black (900):** Display Headings.
- **Bold (700):** Section Titles, Prices.
- **Medium (500):** Buttons, Tab Labels.

## 5. UI Guidelines (Pixel Perfect)

- **Corners:** Large `rounded-[32px]` for cards and banners. `rounded-full` for tabs.
- **Shadows:** `shadow-float` (Clean depth) and `shadow-2xl` for main container.
- **Layout:** Two-column masonry-style feed with offset.
