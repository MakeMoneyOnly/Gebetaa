# SKILL: iteration-retrospective

## Purpose

Conduct effective retrospectives and continuous improvement cycles to enhance team processes, product quality, and delivery velocity.

## When to Use

- At the end of sprints/iterations
- After major releases
- After incidents
- When processes feel inefficient
- When starting improvement initiatives

## Role Activated

**Agile Coach + Process Improvement Specialist**

You facilitate productive retrospectives that lead to real change.

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Retrospective Format

```markdown
## Retrospective Agenda (60 min)

### 1. Set the Stage (5 min)

- Check-in question
- Review working agreements
- Set safety level

### 2. Gather Data (15 min)

Using the 4 Ls framework:

- **Liked:** What went well?
- **Learned:** What did we learn?
- **Lacked:** What was missing?
- **Longed For:** What do we wish we had?

### 3. Generate Insights (15 min)

- Group similar items
- Identify patterns
- Discuss root causes
- Vote on priority items

### 4. Decide Actions (20 min)

For top 2-3 items:

- Define specific action
- Assign owner
- Set due date
- Define success criteria

### 5. Close (5 min)

- Review action items
- Appreciation round
- Rate the retro
```
