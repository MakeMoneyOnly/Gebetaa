## 1. Idea Canvas

### Raw Input

A multi-tenant digital menu and restaurant infrastructure platform, specifically tailored for emerging markets (starting with Ethiopia). It aims to replace physical menus to handle frequent price changes due to inflation and provide data-driven tooling to small/medium restaurants.

### Core Insight

Existing enterprise solutions (Toast, Square) are too expensive and heavy for emerging markets. Local restaurants encounter "inflation friction" (reprinting costs) and "data blindness" (no insights). There is a "white space" for a "Lightweight Toast" that is mobile-first, offline-capable, and ultra-simple.

### Problem Hypothesis

- **Whom:** Small to medium restaurants in high-inflation economies (e.g., Ethiopia).
- **Problem:**
    1.  Frequent price changes require expensive menu reprinting ($200-800/batch).
    2.  Lack of data on sales/margins ("gut feeling" operations).
    3.  Inefficient manual upselling by staff.
    4.  Fragmented multi-location management for chains.
- **Context:** Unreliable internet, older smartphones, cash-heavy economy.

### Solution Hypothesis

Build a multi-tenant SaaS platform offering:

1.  **Dynamic QR Menus:** Real-time price updates (no reprinting).
2.  **Smart Upselling:** Simple algorithmic suggestions to boost AOV.
3.  **Menu Intelligence:** Basic "Popular vs Profitable" analytics.
4.  **Emerging Market Tech:** Offline mode, local payments, WhatsApp integration, lightweight PWA.

### Value Hypothesis

Restaurants will pay ($10-50/mo) because we:

1.  Eliminate reprinting costs (immediate ROI).
2.  Increase Average Order Value (AOV) by ~20% via smart upselling.
3.  Provide multi-location control which they currently lack.
